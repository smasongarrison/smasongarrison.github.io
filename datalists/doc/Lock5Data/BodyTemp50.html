BodyTemp50R Documentation

Body Temperatures

Description

Sample of 50 body temperatures

Format

A dataset with 50 observations on the following 3 variables.

BodyTemp Body temperatures in degrees F
Pulse Pulse rates (beats per minute)
Gender F=Female, M=Male

Details

Body temperatures and pulse rates for a sample of 50 healthy adults.

Source

Shoemaker, What's Normal: Temperature, Gender and Heartrate, Journal of Statistics Education, Vol. 4, No. 2 (1996)
