hypoxiaR Documentation

Gene Expression Under Hypoxia/Normoxia

Description

Gene expression of MCF7 cells under conditions of hypoxia and normoxia.

Usage

data(hypoxia)

Format

A data frame with 1009 observations on 5 variables.

freq

cDNA frequency (numeric, 0-1)

gene

Gene (factor)

rx

Treatment condition (factor)

comp.conc

Concentration of competitor (numeric, M)

flag

Exclude data point (numeric, 0 or 1)
