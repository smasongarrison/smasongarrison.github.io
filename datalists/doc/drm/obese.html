obeseR Documentation

Obesity among Muscatine children

Description

Data of the completers concerning the presence or abscence of obesity in five age cohorts in Muscatine, Ohio, measured repeatedly in -77, -79 and -81.

Usage

data(obese)

Format

A data frame with 5310 observations on the following 5 variables.

id

identification number of a child

obese

a binary indicator of obesity

sex

a factor with levels male female

year

year of measurement

age

a factor indicating age cohorts from (5,7] to (17,19]

Source

Woolson RF and Clarke WR. Analysis of categorical incomplete longitudinal data. Journal of the Royal Statistical Society Series A, 1984; 147:87-99.

http://www.biostat.harvard.edu/~fitzmaur/ala/obesity.txt
