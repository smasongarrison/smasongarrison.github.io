wreathR Documentation

Data Simulated from a 14-Component Mixture

Description

A dataset consisting of 1000 observations drawn from a 14-component normal mixture in which the covariances of the components have the same size and shape but differ in orientation.

Usage

data(wreath)

References

C. Fraley, A. E. Raftery and R. Wehrens (2005). Incermental model-based clustering for large datasets with small clusters. Journal of Computational and Graphical Statistics 14:1:18.

C. Fraley, A. E. Raftery, T. B. Murphy and L. Scrucca (2012). mclust Version 4 for R: Normal Mixture Modeling for Model-Based Clustering, Classification, and Density Estimation. Technical Report No. 597, Department of Statistics, University of Washington.
