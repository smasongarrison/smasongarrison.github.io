Welcome

My name is Mason Garrison. I am a Nation Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellow and Quantitative Methods graduate student at Vanderbilt University, Peabody College in Nashville, Tennessee. Recently, I returned from being a visiting international researcher at the University of British Columbia, where I worked with Prof. Jeremy Biesanz and Prof. Anita DeLongis.

My research uses kinship structure, such as married partners or siblings to infer causation about individual differences. I am particularly interested in developing kinship models to explore the relationship amoung personality, intelligence, and health.
